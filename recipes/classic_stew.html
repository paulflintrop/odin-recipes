Classic stew

Description

A delicious stew, better if it takes its time to simmer.

  • Potatoes
  • Dried meat
  • Courgette
  • Red bell pepper
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  1. Bake the dried meat and onion with some paprika spice and tomato extract.
  2. Add the courgette and red bell pepper
  3. Add water and potatoes
  4. Let simmer for as long as you are patient
