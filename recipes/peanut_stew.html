Peanut stew

Description

A vegan stew with a strong taste of peanut. An ideal recipe to meal prep as it stays well in the fridge or freezer. Can be combined with bread or rice.

  • Onion
  • Sweet potaties
  • Blocks of tomato
  • Spinach
  • Chickpeas
  1. Bake the onion and add the spices
  2. Add the sweet potatoes
  3. Add the blocks of tomato and water to be able to cook the sweet potatoes
  4. Once the potatoes are cooked add the chickpeas and spinach
  5. Let it simmer and prep for serving
