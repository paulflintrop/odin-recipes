Pasta pesto

Description

A quick and easy recipe to create pasta pesto

  • Pasta
  • Bacon
  • Courgette
  • Mushrooms
  • Pesto
  1. Cook the pasta
  2. While the pasta is cooking bake the bacon
  3. Add the mushrooms to the bacon
  4. Add the courgette
  5. Finally add the pesto and cooked pasta to finish the meal
